The Sun

The Sun is a G-type main-sequence star (a yellow dwarf) at the heart of our Solar System. It is a near-perfect sphere of hot plasma, generating light and heat through nuclear fusion of hydrogen into helium in its core.

  • Diameter: 1,391,000 km (about 109 Earths across)
  • Surface temp: ~5,500 °C
  • Core temp: ~15,000,000 °C
  • Mass: 333,000 × Earth's mass
  • Age: ~4.6 billion years
